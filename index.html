Welcome to Tofu's stationery shop! Here, we provide the high end stationery with the most suitable prices. One of the reasons we're famous for would definitely be our infamous backstory and how we came about. It all started when Prof. K-cube came from Mars to conduct a research on human beings 8 decades ago. Upon asking for a pen and a notebook from his partner Prof. Min Set, he was quite disappointed by the quality of stationery on Earth. Then, his student Aye Myo suggested making his own customised stationery to use on Earth, since having an unsatisfied Prof. wouldn't do good to their research. The three of them then went to search for the best crafter on Earth and finally scouted phoo mya, who was known for being the best on Earth. When the stationeries came out, prof K-cube was ecstatic and decided to share the products with the world by selling and distributing them. One night, after their weekly meetings, he stumbled upon a mapo tofu shop named "MON's" and he decided to give it a try. AND WOW! he had never had this "tofu" thing before in his life and it was out of the world, according to him. Right after that, he contacted his team and informed them that he would be naming the store TOFU. Everyone was baffled, but went along with him and that's how the best stationery brand came about. Although his research went into the drain, he was able to provide people with these stationeries. Seeing how we're still here in this day and age, just shows how much people love our products. Enjoy your time exploring here!
